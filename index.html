Frequently asked questions

What problems do you solve?

Talkie enables lightweight, spontaneous collaboration for staying connected. Our mission is to make remote communication frictions less & more expressive. A remote team does not have to feel like one.

Do I need Talkie if we already use zoom/slack?

Talkie is about what happens in-between meetings for spontaneous conversations, feeling connected and lightweight brainstorming.

How do I use Talkie in my workflow?

Join spontaneous conversations, summon people on the fly or send voice notes to your team & avoid too many meetings.
